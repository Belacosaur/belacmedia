## What Changed (Simple Explanation)

### 1) Search Result Quality
We replaced generic page titles/descriptions with clear, page-specific versions.

### 2) Duplicate-Content Protection
We fixed canonical tags so each important page points to itself correctly.

### 3) Better Indexing Control
We prevented low-value/private utility pages from being treated like marketing pages.

### 4) Better Discovery
We aligned sitemap and robots settings so search engines prioritize the right pages.

### 5) Better Social Presentation
We added a proper social preview image and metadata for cleaner shared links.

### 6) Better Search Understanding
We expanded schema markup to better describe the business and website content.
