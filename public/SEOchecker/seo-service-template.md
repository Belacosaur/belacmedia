## 3) What We Fixed (In Plain English)

### A) Page Titles and Descriptions
**Why this matters:** These are what users see in Google results.  
**What we changed:** Added/cleaned unique title and description tags for important pages.  
**Result:** Better clarity in search results and higher click potential.

### B) Canonical URLs (Duplicate-Content Protection)
**Why this matters:** Prevents Google from getting confused by duplicate versions of pages.  
**What we changed:** Added correct canonical rules per page.  
**Result:** Stronger page authority and cleaner indexing.

### C) Indexing Rules (What Google Should/Shouldn’t Index)
**Why this matters:** Private/utility pages should not compete with sales pages.  
**What we changed:** Added noindex rules for non-marketing pages and cleaned crawl policy.  
**Result:** Crawl budget is focused on pages that actually drive business.

### D) Sitemap + Robots
**Why this matters:** Helps search engines discover and prioritize your key pages.  
**What we changed:** Updated sitemap and robots logic to match live page strategy.  
**Result:** Faster and more reliable page discovery.

### E) Social Preview Metadata
**Why this matters:** Link shares on social platforms look more professional and clickable.  
**What we changed:** Added proper social card image and metadata.  
**Result:** Better visual quality and click-through from social shares.

### F) Structured Data (Schema)
**Why this matters:** Gives search engines context about your business and pages.  
**What we changed:** Added/expanded schema markup (Organization/WebSite/[Service Type]).  
**Result:** Stronger trust and clearer content understanding for search engines.
